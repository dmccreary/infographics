## About This MicroSim

This two-stage quiz tests your ability to classify learning objectives by Bloom's Taxonomy level and then match each level to the most appropriate MicroSim interaction pattern. The quiz presents 10 learning objectives drawn from this textbook, each containing a key action verb that signals its cognitive level.

## Lesson Plan

### Learning Objective

Differentiate between the six Bloom's Taxonomy levels by matching learning objectives to the correct level and selecting the most appropriate MicroSim interaction pattern for each.

### Activities

1. Complete all 10 questions and review your score.
2. For any incorrect answers, note the key verb and explanation.
3. Reset and try again — aim for 10/10.
