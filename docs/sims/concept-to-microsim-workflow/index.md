## About This MicroSim

This interactive workflow diagram shows the five stages of creating a MicroSim with AI assistance. Each stage is labeled with who drives the work — **HUMAN**, **AI + REVIEW**, or **AUTOMATED** — highlighting where pedagogical judgment is essential and where AI accelerates execution.

## Lesson Plan

### Learning Objective

Describe the five stages of the concept-to-MicroSim AI-assisted workflow and explain what happens at each stage, including where human judgment is required versus where AI handles execution.

### Activities

1. Click each stage to read its description, inputs, outputs, and tools.
2. Enable **Show Time Comparison** to see AI-assisted vs. manual timing for each stage.
3. Identify the two stages that are fully human-driven. Why can't AI replace human judgment there?
4. Calculate the total time for AI-assisted vs. manual workflow. What is the speedup factor?
