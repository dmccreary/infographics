## About This MicroSim

This interactive timeline traces the evolution of AI-assisted infographic creation across five eras, from template-based tools (2015) to projected autonomous curriculum design (2030). A "We Are Here" indicator marks 2026, helping learners orient themselves in the progression. Each era reveals the tools available, the educator's role, and the key limitations of that period.

## Lesson Plan

### Learning Objective

Examine the evolution of AI-assisted infographic creation from early template-based tools through current LLM-powered generation to projected future capabilities, identifying how each advancement changes the educator's role.

### Activities

1. Click each era marker to compare tools, time-per-infographic, and output quality.
2. Enable **Show Role Comparison** to see how human vs. AI effort shifts across eras.
3. Identify the turning point where AI effort exceeds human effort. What enabled that shift?
4. Discuss: What role should educators always retain, even as AI capabilities increase?
